CHAIN-OF-THOUGHT PROMPT

Problem:

A laptop costs ₹50,000.
A store offers a 20% discount.

Find:

1. Discount amount
2. Final price

Prompt:

Think step by step and show all calculations before giving the final answer.

Expected Reasoning:

Step 1:
20% of ₹50,000

Step 2:
0.20 × 50,000

Step 3:
Discount = ₹10,000

Step 4:
Final Price = ₹50,000 − ₹10,000

Answer:
₹40,000
